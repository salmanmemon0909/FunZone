Pygmy Goat

The African Pygmy goat is known for its ability to survive on poor pasture. They are vegetarians that prefer to browse rather than graze. To reach high branches they stand on their hind legs. Both sexes of African Pygmy goats have horns.

FAST FACTS

SCIENTIFIC NAME CAPRA HIRCUS

WHERE IN THE WILD? ORIGINATED IN THE CAMEROON VALLEY OF WEST AFRICA

WHAT'S ON THE MENU? ENJOY VARIETY IN DIET, INCLUDING WOODY PLANTS, HAY , OATS, PELLETS

LIFE EXPECTANCY 10-18 YEARS
