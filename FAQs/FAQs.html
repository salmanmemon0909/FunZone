Fun zone Amusement park

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a safe and enjoyable experience while visiting Fun zone Amusement park and remember the following:

  • DO NOT enter into any restricted areas including ride perimeters and designated employee areas.
  • Enter the park through designated entrances during operating hours only.
  • Use designated walkways throughout the park. Do not cross any fences, chains, landscape areas or other barriers.
  • Be attentive to your surroundings. Watch where you are walking. Walk, don’t run
  • Be sure to follow all rider requirements, including requirements for loose articles.

Tickets & Discounts

We do not have a limited activity ticket. Fun Zone tickets include all rides, shows (except special ticketed events), museums, crafts and other attractions (except for our paid games).

Yes, we offer group rates for groups of 15 or more paying Guests. Our group reservation number is 000-0000. Groups must make reservations at least 2 weeks in advance in order to receive one (1) complimentary ticket per fifteen (15) paid. Please see our Groups section for more information.

If you have had a season pass within the previous 3 years, you are eligible to renew that season pass but may not qualify for renewal pricing. If it has been 4 or more years, you must purchase a new season pass. If you have received a refund on your pass or have a PreK pass, you will need to purchase a new season pass.

Season Passes are your best value if visiting more than one time during the season. You can apply your paid one-day admission toward the price of a season pass. This transaction must be made on the day of your visit before leaving the Park.

General Park Info

While you are with us, you are expected to comply with the following*:

  • Respect each other as well as our employees; verbal abuse of other guests or our employees will not be tolerated.
  • Following updated guidance from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), fully vaccinated guests can now visit Dollywood without wearing a mask or physically distancing. Additionally, consistent with state guidelines, guests aged 12 and under may also visit without wearing a mask or physically distancing.
  • Refrain from fighting, horseplay, taunting or threatening remarks & gestures
  • Do not create disruption with behavior that is unruly, disruptive or illegal.
  • Weapons are not allowed on the premises, including knives with blades longer than 3 1/2 inches.
  • Follow instructions from employees regarding operations and procedures.

Any Guest who fails to comply with the Terms of Sale, including without limitation those listed above, may at the discretion of FUN ZONE have their Ticket revoked or confiscated by FUN ZONE here with no refund of any amount paid therefor. Upon such revocation or confiscation, the applicable Guest and all members of their party shall have no right to remain in, on, or about the Property. FUN ZONE reserves the right to ban such individuals from future admission to all or any of its Properties or from participation in any Herschend Family Entertainment Activities.

Directions & Parking

Saima Trade Tower, B-115, 1st Floor، I.I Chundrigar Rd, Karachi, Pakistan

Parking is 100 PKR for all standard length vehicles, 50PKR for oversize length vehicles such as RVs and trailers. However, parking is FREE if you purchase a Fun Zone Gold or Diamond Season Pass. Retroactive benefits such as FREE parking do not apply if you upgrade to Gold or Diamond after the purchase of a Season Pass.

You will receive a parking permit when you pay 90PKR at the tollbooth. This allows you to park your RV in our "Permit Parking" lot. Trams provide transportation to the Front Gate. RV parking lot is not available for overnight parking or camping.

Accessibility Information

Yes. Disability license plate or hang tag must be visible.Standard parking fees apply. Along with ample disability parking, Fun Zone Theme Park also provides accessible trams for those using wheelchairs during their visit.

A majority of the Park is level to gently-rolling and is thus wheelchair accessible.

Tips For Visiting With Children

Fun Zone offers two Baby Care Centers for the convenience of our guests in the Cascades and the Big Bear Plunge areas. Each facility offers a climate-controlled environment with:

  • One glider and foot stool
  • Baby changing station
  • Sink

For the safety of our guests, children must be 13 years old or older to enter without an adult. Children 12 years old and younger must be accompanied by a chaperone 16 years old or older. Unescorted children will not be admitted to funzone park.

Life vests are provided at no additional charge and will be required for non-swimmers and small children at the direction of the Lifeguards. Personal life vests must be USCG approved and in good condition. Water wings, flotation seats or bubble packs may not be used. Life vests are strongly recommended for weak swimmers.

Swim diapers must be worn by babies / toddlers not yet potty trained. Swim diapers are available at the Riverside Trading Post.

Park Policies

If you smoke or vape, please utilize the many designated smoking and vaping areas indicated on the Guide Map. Smoking and vaping is strictly forbidden on all rides, queue lines, shops and restaurants.

Distracting tattoos should be covered when entering the park. Management reserves the right to ask for distracting tattoos to be covered at their discretion.

Weapons and alcoholic beverages are not permitted on Dollywood's Splash Country Property.

  • Commercial photography/videography must be approved in advance by Fun Zone Public Relations Team and approved vendors must be escorted throughout the shoot.
  • No on-slide video or pictures are permitted.
  • Unless approved by Fun Zone in advance, personal or commercial drones (or other unmanned vehicles) whether piloted on or off property are not permitted.
