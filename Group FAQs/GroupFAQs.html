Group FAQs

Group rates are available for groups of 15 or more with at least a 72 hour advance reservation. Picnics must have a minimum of 15 and require a two week notice. Groups not pre-ticketed must enter the park together with a one payment transaction. Sorry, individual will-call tickets cannot be arranged. Tickets can be sent to you in advance for group distribution prior to arrival at the park when contract is paid in full. Your final pricing per ticket will be based on all tickets delivered to you minus any tickets you return to us (must purchase a minimum of 15 tickets). Returned tickets must be received by our finance department within two weeks after your event.

Yes, by advance arrangement only. A 25% non-refundable deposit is required and credit must be pre-approved. Contact us for more information.

Yes, the group leader can get additional tickets at the group sales ticket window; however, in order to expedite your group's entry, we suggest that you take every endeavor to order the exact number of tickets you will be using.

Group meals are available for groups of 15 or more with at least two weeks prior notice and a 25% deposit. The group must commit to a minimum number of meals at least 72 hours prior to the event. Food and beverage services will be prepared for the guaranteed number. All charges will be based on the guaranteed number of meals or the actual number of meals served, whichever is greater. Colored wristbands are provided in advance for distribution to all meal recipients and worn upon entry to the park. Bands are not to be removed.

For the safety of our guests, swimsuits are not allowed in fun zone. Swimsuits can only be worn in water park.

Funzone Theme Park and Water Park are pay-one-price parks: two parks for one price. All rides, shows and attractions are included in the price of admission (parking, games, food and merchandise are exempt)
